About Abigail's Pet Shop


We are a women owned small pet shop. All of our animals are taken care of to the highest of standards, and all are up to date on shots and vet visits. Cats and dogs will be spayed or neutered before coming to your home! Check out our selection and we're sure you'll find the perfect pet for you!
